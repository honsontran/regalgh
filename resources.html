NLC is not only a support agency. We want to help you in multitide of ways. Click on each service for more information.

Food Stamps (NJSNAP)

Need help putting food on the table? Don't know how or if you can pay for your next meal? You might qualify for NJ's food stamps program. Find out if you are eligible!

Energy Assistance (LIHEAP)

Cold winters? Can't afford gas or electricity? The Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP) helps families reduce the cost of their utility bills for more affordable living.
 

Learn More

General Assistance (Workfirst NJ)

These benefits include, but are not limited to: food, clothing, shelter and household furnishings; rental assistance or mortgage payments; utility payments; transportation to search for housing; and moving expenses.

Learn More

Unemployment Benefits

Having trouble finding a new job? If you have been laid off at no fault on your own the last 20 weeks, you may be eligible to receive unemployment benefits. Click learn more to find out about how you can file for benefits today.                           

Learn More

Health Insurance   (NJ Medicaid)

Medicaid provides health insurance to parents/caretakers, dependent children, pregnant women, elders, blind or the disabled. These programs pay for hospital services, doctor visits, prescriptions, nursing home care and other healthcare needs.

Learn More

Transportation Assistance

No car? Need help getting to work? If you take public transportation, you may receive a one-time free monthly bus or train pass from NJ TRANSIT. You may also be eligible for additional passes as well. Click below to learn more.
