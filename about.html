About Us

Whether you need a home health aide or employment, our professionals connect you with providers of those services.

 NLC is a support agency.

Choosing a support coordination agency is a new experience for a majority of people. Support Coordinators here at NLC make it possible for families to match the person's wants and needs with skills and expertise. Since people are not aware of what is available in their community, we help you find providers and services that are in your area. Click below to view some frequently asked questions in regards to NLC or Support Agencies in general.

Frequently Asked Questions

We care about the power to choose what you want.

It is important that you should select a provider of services that you feel comfortable with in these matters as well. Do this by discussing the support coordination services you need and letting us know what you expect from us. We believe in great communication with those we serve. Finally, you should feel free to change to a different provider agency when the need arises. If are working with a provider of services, let your support coordinator know immediately.

Personalized Services? We got you covered.

Our Support Coordinators realize how critical it is to have services that are personalized and unique to you. Choosing the best services for you could be the difference between success and failure. We do not provide these services, but we ensure we call a multitude of services to ensure the best fit.
